### Typography

### Spacing
- Base unit: 4pt
- Standard content margin: 16pt horizontal
- Card padding: 16pt
- Section spacing: 24pt

### Corner Radius
- Cards: 12pt
- Buttons: 10pt
- Inputs: 8pt
- Chips/Tags: 6pt

### Animations
- Standard transition: `easeInOut(duration: 0.25)`
- Spring feedback: `spring(duration: 0.4, bounce: 0.2)`
- Must respect `accessibilityReduceMotion`
